Privacy and Policy for Kanye Bot by The One Who Invents

The Premissions of the bot gives it the power to:
1. Send messages (including in Threads)
2. Send Text-To-Speech messages
3. Read message History
4. Connect
5. Speak
6. Use voice activity

  By allowing the bot to do that he'll be able to only send messages, connect to voice chat and play audio without recording. With message history he'll be able to replay to messages. The bot has the abillity to show ping. It does not have any way of getting information about users.
